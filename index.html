About Me

Seton is a soon-to-be full-stack developer who lives in Chapel Hill with her husband and her two cats. She attended University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, where she earned a Bachelor's in English Language and Literature. She has spent the last four years teaching high school in Sanford, N.C. Teaching has definitely been a great learning experience, but she's ready for something different.

Seton's best skills include coding, writing, and editing. She is an avid reader and moviegoer. She would somehow love to combine all these interests into one fabulous career in web development!
